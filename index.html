Hello! I am Sourabha. I am a Software Engineer at D. E. Shaw India, developing full stack applications that provide insights on the Human Capital data. I did my B. Tech (Honours) in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Bhubaneswar.

During my undergraduate, I was closely working under Dr. Sudipta Saha on projects spun across multiple areas such as WSN, Computer Vision, X11 Window systems, Robotics and Image Processing. I was also associated with his research group Decentralized and Smart Systems Research Group (DSSRG) where I contributed to developing and testing energy efficient synchronous communication based protocols. We recently also published a paper on robotics titled 'Concurrent Transmission for Multi-Robot Coordination' which has been accepted at the IEEE CCNC 2022 RoboCom Workshop.

In my spare time, I spend a good amount of time in the garden. I strongly believe that plants can be good friends :) I also love cooking and sketching.
Biology also interests me, and sometimes read articles on different fields -- mostly on behaviour and intelligence of insects (Of course, our best friend, Drosophilla!).

Publications

Concurrent Transmission for Multi-Robot Coordination - arXiv

IEEE CCNC 2022 RoboCom Workshop
Mini Abstract
We propose a replacement to the existing asynchronous communication protocol in swarm robotic systems with the concurrent communication protocol that is more reliable, energy efficient and fast. To solve the portability issue of concurrent protocol hardware, we propose a split-architecure, dividing the robotic architecture into independent communication and computation units, that interact through a simple serial line protocol.
